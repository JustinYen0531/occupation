# ETP Motto Oral Sharing｜每頁口說關鍵字提示

這份不是逐字稿，而是讓你看到投影片時，能立刻想起「這一頁我要分享什麼」。

使用方式：

1. 先記住每頁的「關鍵字」。
2. 主旨句只要記住意思，不必逐字背。
3. 延伸點依現場感覺選一至兩個說，不必全部講完。
4. 忘詞時直接回到關鍵字，重新說一次自己的感受即可。

---

## Page 1｜Opening

**關鍵字：Journey（旅程）**

**主旨句：**

> Today, I want to share how my motto gradually became a real method in my life.

**可以自由延伸：**

- 這不只是一句漂亮的 motto，而是這學期真的反覆使用的方法。
- 一開始只是想讓困難的事情比較容易開始，後來它影響了做專案、比賽和面對壓力的方式。
- 今天不是要報告每個專案細節，而是分享自己的想法怎麼改變。

**接下一頁：**

> So, let me start from the moment when I first tried to put this motto into action.

---

## Page 2｜Weeks 4–5：Starting the Engine

**關鍵字：Just Start（先開始）／Lower the Barrier（降低門檻）**

**主旨句 1：**

> At the beginning, my biggest progress was not becoming independent. It was becoming willing to start and ask for help.

**主旨句 2：**

> I also learned that deadlines are not always enemies. If I start early, they can become tools that push me forward.

**可以自由延伸：**

- Stock Ski：把「能看」的股票分析變成「能玩」，代表自己開始敢把想法做出來。
- Classroom game：即使時間短、很多東西還不熟，仍然可以跟著步驟先完成原型。
- 當時依賴別人的協助並不可恥，因為願意求助本身就是一次突破。
- 自己不擅長在最後一刻承受壓力，所以「提早開始」比硬撐更適合自己。

**接下一頁：**

> Once I learned how to start, the next challenge was facing something I did not understand at all.

---

## Page 3｜Weeks 6–7：FHIR-RouRou Build & Sprint

**關鍵字：Curiosity（好奇）／Manageable（可以處理）／Perfectionism（完美主義）**

**主旨句 1：**

> FHIR looked extremely abstract at first, but asking the same question many times slowly made it understandable.

**主旨句 2：**

> I kept myself moving by treating the competition as an interesting experiment, not as a test I had to win.

**主旨句 3：**

> Sometimes I am not lazy. I care so much about the result that perfectionism makes the task feel heavier than it really is.

**可以自由延伸：**

- 從 Dify、Flowise 到 Node engine，技術一直改，但自己沒有因此停下來。
- 不把得獎設成唯一目標，反而更容易進入 flow state。
- 已經做出原型後仍然覺得不夠好，讓自己看起來很狼狽；真正的壓力常來自「一定要完美」。
- Motto 的作用不是否認困難，而是把抽象的恐懼變成下一個可以問、可以做的小步驟。

**接下一頁：**

> But understanding one difficult project was very different from handling many projects at the same time.

---

## Page 4｜Weeks 8–9：Midterm Storm

**關鍵字：Priorities（取捨）／Pressure（壓力）／Good Enough（夠好）**

**主旨句 1：**

> This was the week when I realized that making everything manageable also means accepting that not everything can be completed.

**主旨句 2：**

> My goal was not to make every project perfect, but to protect the most valuable parts and keep moving.

**主旨句 3：**

> Compared with my past self, I could finally stay functional under pressure, even when the result was not ideal.

**可以自由延伸：**

- FHIR 決賽、ETP Game Show、Mine-Chess、Stock Ski 同時進行，壓力不是想像，而是真的很多事撞在一起。
- 把每個專案分配到不同天，讓「暴風雨」變成每天一件普通的小事。
- 以前可能會因為做不完而直接崩潰；這次可以接受低一點的分數，也願意嘗試新東西。
- 後來發現不只是要早開始，也要承認時間有限，做出選擇。

**接下一頁：**

> After surviving that storm, I began to think more seriously about strategy, not only effort.

---

## Page 5｜Weeks 10–11：Finals & Park Mode

**關鍵字：Strategy（策略）／Small Bites（切小塊）／Imperfect（不完美）**

**主旨句 1：**

> Stock Ski became a concrete version of my motto: it invites people into something difficult and helps them discover that it is not as frightening as it looks.

**主旨句 2：**

> My real problem was not simply procrastination. I had expected myself to build a mature product and finish everything at the same time.

**主旨句 3：**

> A checklist and clear priorities turned a desperate five-day deadline into a series of small, manageable steps.

**可以自由延伸：**

- Park Mode 把分析包成任務、地圖和角色，正好呼應「降低開始的門檻」。
- 自己開始理解工程思維不只是會寫程式，而是知道怎麼排序、拆解和交付。
- 每天告訴自己「今天的成果已經是 good work」，是在對抗完美主義，不是在放棄品質。
- 為課堂需求做合適的版本，比硬把每件作品都做成競賽級產品更實際。

**接下一頁：**

> Then I tried to bring the same method into a completely new field, but this time I could not work alone.

---

## Page 6｜Weeks 11–13：Teamwork & Closing

**關鍵字：Transfer（遷移）／Ask（開口）／Teamwork（合作）**

**主旨句 1：**

> I stopped seeing a new competition as starting from zero. I treated it as transferring a workflow I had already learned.

**主旨句 2：**

> The most difficult part was not building the app. It was asking other people to join me.

**主旨句 3：**

> My motto does not remove every challenge; it helps me decide when to lower the barrier and when to step outside my comfort zone.

**可以自由延伸：**

- 先釐清規則、目標和資料格式，再做 UI，表示自己已經開始有一套可重複的方法。
- 原本以為招募隊友要寫很正式的長文，後來發現一句真誠的邀請就可以開始。
- AI 很會完成任務，但比賽也逼自己學習真正的人際合作。
- 做 app 已經慢慢成為舒適圈，所以接下來還要學習平衡專案與課業。

**接下一頁：**

> These projects may sound like separate events, so I made a short video to show the whole journey together.

---

## Page 7｜My Progress Video

**關鍵字：Evidence（證明）／Transformation（轉變）**

**播放前主旨句：**

> This video is not only a collection of projects. It shows how I moved from hesitation to action.

**播放後可以補一句：**

> When I look back, the biggest change is not how much I built, but how differently I now respond to difficulty.

**觀看時心裡抓三件事：**

- 我從哪裡開始？
- 哪些作品代表我的轉變？
- 現在的我和學期初有什麼不同？

**接下一頁：**

> However, I did not want to understand this change only from my own perspective.

---

## Page 8｜My Interview Video

**關鍵字：Other Perspectives（別人的角度）／Confirmation（印證）**

**播放前主旨句：**

> I interviewed other people because I wanted to know whether my personal method also made sense outside my own experience.

**播放後可以補一句：**

> Their answers did not give me one perfect formula, but they confirmed several directions I had already started to discover.

**觀看時心裡抓四件事：**

- 降低開始門檻。
- 把任務拆小。
- 改變環境或使用適度壓力。
- 願意向外尋求協助。

**接下一頁：**

> After comparing their answers, I found four ideas that connected strongly with my own experience.

---

## Page 9｜Interview Content Discussion

**關鍵字：Mirror（鏡子）／Four Supports（四種支撐）**

**主旨句 1：**

> The interviews worked like a mirror: they helped me see that my personal habits were actually repeatable strategies.

**主旨句 2：**

> The four strategies were lowering the barrier, breaking tasks down, creating useful pressure, and seeking external support.

**主旨句 3：**

> The most important confirmation was that asking for help is not disturbing others; it is part of solving a difficult problem.

**可以自由延伸：**

- Michael：換環境，例如去圖書館，讓自己比較容易專注。
- Aia：把壓力當成行動力，但這種方法不一定適合每個時刻。
- Ming 和 Justin：完成里程碑後給自己小獎勵。
- 訪談沒有推翻自己的 motto，而是讓它變得更完整、更具體。

**接下一頁：**

> So after the projects and interviews, I asked myself one final question: what should I keep, and what should I change?

---

## Page 10｜Changes to My Motto：Reflection

**關鍵字：Keep（保留）／Adjust（調整）／Practice（實踐）**

**主旨句 1：**

> I would keep the courage to believe that difficult things are conquerable.

**主旨句 2：**

> What I would change is the belief that everything must be finished. Making things manageable also requires priorities.

**主旨句 3：**

> I would practice the motto through checklists, smaller steps, and earlier preparation, not only through positive thinking.

**可以自由延伸：**

- 保留：好奇、願意嘗試、不只為了得獎。
- 調整：不是所有事情都要做到滿分，先保住最重要的部分。
- 實踐：文件、優先順序、每日進度，讓 motto 從心態變成方法。
- 可以用這句收尾：**Not perfect, but fine, isn’t it?**

**接下一頁：**

> To make this reflection practical, I turned it into five actions for my future self.

---

## Page 11｜Five Future Actions

**關鍵字：Mission Plan（未來任務）**

**主旨句：**

> My revised motto is no longer just “believe it is easy.” It is a five-step mission plan for how I will act next time.

**五個動作只記五個字：**

1. **Earlier**：提早開始，避免截止壓力破壞表現。
2. **Prioritize**：依重要性和難度排序，再拆成小步驟。
3. **Accept**：接受不完美的進度，先完成最有價值的部分。
4. **Independent**：練習獨立規畫，也減少猶豫不決。
5. **Explore**：繼續嘗試新事物，把每個專案當成學習機會。

**可以自由延伸：**

- 不用逐項講得一樣長，挑自己最需要改善的兩點多分享。
- 建議特別講 **Prioritize** 和 **Accept**，因為它們是這學期後半段最明顯的新體悟。

**接下一頁：**

> That is the end of my journey, but I hope it can also become the beginning of a conversation.

---

## Page 12｜Questions

**關鍵字：Conversation（對話）**

**主旨句：**

> Thank you for listening. I would be happy to hear your questions or your own way of making difficult things manageable.

**如果現場安靜，可以主動丟一題：**

> When you face a difficult task, do you usually need less pressure, or do you need more pressure to start?

這樣就不只是被動說「Any questions?」，而是真的把分享交給觀眾。

---

## Page 13｜Takeaway Message

**關鍵字：Light（光）／Being Seen（被看見）**

**主旨句 1：**

> My final takeaway is that growth does not mean never breaking down. Sometimes we understand ourselves only after going through a difficult period.

**主旨句 2：**

> The light matters because there was darkness before it, and our progress becomes meaningful because we know what it took to reach it.

**可以自由延伸：**

- 這學期的壓力、猶豫和不完美，不需要從故事裡刪掉，它們正是成長有意義的原因。
- Motto 不是要假裝黑暗不存在，而是讓自己在黑暗裡仍願意往光的方向走一步。
- 最後可以停一下，再說：

> Not perfect, but fine. And still moving forward.

---

# 最後只背這條故事線

**Journey → Start → Curiosity → Priorities → Strategy → Teamwork → Evidence → Perspectives → Mirror → Reflect → Act → Conversation → Light**

中文版本：

**旅程 → 開始 → 好奇 → 取捨 → 策略 → 合作 → 證明 → 他人視角 → 印證 → 反思 → 行動 → 對話 → 光**

如果台上突然空白，不要努力搜尋原句，只要問自己：

> 「這一頁的關鍵字是什麼？它跟我發生過的哪件事有關？」

只要能回答這兩題，你就不是在背稿，而是真的在分享。
